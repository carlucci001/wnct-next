# Content Management

## Articles
Navigate to **Articles** to manage news stories.
- **Create**: Click "New Article".
- **Edit**: Click the pencil icon on an article row.
- **Status**: Articles can be Draft, Published, or Archived.
- **Categories**: Assign categories like News, Sports, Weather.

## Newsletters
Navigate to **Newsletters** to manage email campaigns.
- **Create**: Use the drag-and-drop editor.
- **Send**: Send immediately or schedule for later.
- **Subscribers**: View and manage the mailing list.

## Categories
Manage article categories in **Settings > Categories**.
- Define name, slug, and color.
- Categories appear in the main navigation.

## Comments
Moderate user comments in **Comments**.
- Approve, Reject, or Delete comments.
- Configure moderation rules in Settings.

## Media Library
Navigate to **Media** to manage images and files.
- **Upload**: Drag and drop images.
- **Alt Text**: Add descriptions for accessibility.
- **Search**: Find images by name or date.
